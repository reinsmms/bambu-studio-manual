Prepare – Supports & Surface Tools
Paint supports and other surface tools (including fuzzy skin painting).
Bambu Studio: 2.4.0.70 Verified on: 2025-11-28 Profile: A1 · No AMS
Paint Support tool (controls + colors)
Support painting is fast once you memorize the controls and what each color means.
  • Left click: Enforce supports (purple)
  • Right click: Block supports (red)
  • Shift + left click: Erase painting (returns to default / grey)
  • Ctrl + mouse wheel: Change pen size
  • Cyan: typically indicates contact region at the plate / interface highlight

Practical tip

Think “paint where supports are allowed.” Purple adds support. Red forbids support. Grey removes your paint so the slicer decides again.

Fuzzy Skin painting tool (Fill, Smart Fill Angle, Section View)
Fuzzy Skin painting lets you apply texture only where you want it, without changing the whole model.
Smart Fill Angle

Limits what the Fill tool considers “connected” based on surface angle. Smaller angles stay on similar faces; larger angles spill around corners.

Section View

Lets you cut the view to paint interior or hidden surfaces without rotating endlessly.

Brush vs Fill
  • Brush: you paint exactly where the effect applies.
  • Fill: flood-fills connected surfaces (controlled by Smart Fill Angle).
